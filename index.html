A watch is a portable timepiece intended to be carried
or worn by a person.A wristwatch is designed to be
worn around the wrist, attached by a watch strap

BRACELETS

A bracelet is an article of jewellery that is
worn around the wrist.Bracelets can be
manufactured from metal, leather, cloth,
plastic.

EARRINGS

An earring is a piece of jewelry attached to the ear in
earlobe.Earrings have been worn by people in different
civilizations and historic periods, often with cultural
significance.

ANKLETS

An anklet, also called ankle chain, ankle bracelet or
ankle string,s an ornament worn around the ankle.

and women may wear casual leather anklets.

made from metal or plastic and sometimes feature
decorative fabric.
